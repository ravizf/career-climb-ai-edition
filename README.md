# 🎮 Career Climb: AI Snakes & Ladders

An interactive and modern reimagination of the classic Snakes & Ladders game — transformed into a **career simulation experience**.

Instead of just playing a game, you experience a journey of **growth, decisions, and setbacks**, just like a real student career.

---

## 🚀 Live Concept

🪜 **Ladders** = Smart career moves  
🐍 **Snakes** = Mistakes & setbacks  
🤖 **AI Opponent** = Compete with an automated player  
🧠 **Decision System** = Your choices affect your progress  

---

## ✨ Features

- 🎲 Dice roll animation
- 🧍 Player vs AI gameplay
- 🪜 Ladder climb animations
- 🐍 Snake drop animations
- 🔔 Real-time event notifications
- 🧠 Choice-based decision system
- 📊 XP & Level progression
- 🏆 Best score tracking (localStorage)
- 📱 Responsive UI design
- 🎨 Modern glassmorphism UI
- 🔊 Sound effects for interactions

---

## 🧠 Game Idea

This project reimagines Snakes & Ladders as a **career journey simulator**:

- Ladders represent:
  - Building projects
  - Learning new skills
  - Getting internships

- Snakes represent:
  - Procrastination
  - Burnout
  - Missed deadlines

👉 The goal is to reach position 100 before the AI by making smart decisions.
